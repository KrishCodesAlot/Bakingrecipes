Simple Baking

Chocolate Cake

Flour: 2 cups, Sugar: 1.5 cups, Cocoa: 0.5 cup, Eggs: 2, Milk: 1 cup, Oil: 0.5 cup, Baking Powder: 2 tsp.

Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes.

Instructions: Mix dry, mix wet, combine, bake.

Apple Pie

Apples: 6, Flour: 2.5 cups, Butter: 0.75 cup, Sugar: 0.75 cup, Cinnamon: 1 tsp.

Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 50-60 minutes.

Instructions: Prepare crust, slice apples, mix filling, assemble, bake.

Lemon Cookies

Flour: 2.25 cups, Butter: 1 cup, Sugar: 1 cup, Lemon Zest: 1 tbsp, Lemon Juice: 2 tbsp.

Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10-12 minutes.

Instructions: Cream butter & sugar, add others, chill, bake.
